About EGPAK

Why EGPAK

Digital games have the ability to make academic information for kids more interesting, adaptable, and relevant while also giving teachers quantitative results, according to growing body of research. The EGPAK is of the opinion that more effective projects can reach more students (especially in underperforming schools and underserved communities) by advancing and sharing information about the impact games have on learning, improving producers’ and investors’ capacity to develop commercially viable, research-based educational games, and involving policy makers and practitioners in the discussion

Mission

To ensure world-class education for every learner in Africa through Educational games

Vision

Ensuring that everyone has access to high quality education by creating fun, easy and effective learning environment
